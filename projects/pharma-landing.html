Our Mission

Pioneering Medical Innovation Since 2015

Founded by leading scientists from Harvard Medical School and MIT, Vitalia Pharma emerged from a shared vision: to transform complex biological discoveries into life-changing therapies for patients with limited treatment options.

Our interdisciplinary approach combines cutting-edge biotechnology with computational science to accelerate drug discovery and development.

Our Vision

To become the world's most innovative biopharmaceutical company, delivering breakthrough therapies that address unmet medical needs.

Our Active Research Focus

ONCOLOGY

Precision Immunotherapies

Developing next-generation CAR T-cell therapies and personalized cancer vaccines that harness the body's immune system to target malignancies with unprecedented specificity.

Key Programs:

  • • VT-101: CAR-T for B-cell malignancies (Phase II)
  • • VT-102: Personalized neoantigen vaccine (Phase I)
  • • VT-103: Immune checkpoint modulator (Preclinical)
Pipeline Status: 3 candidates in Phase II trials.
NEUROLOGY

Neurodegenerative Disease Modulation

Investigating small molecules designed to slow or halt the progression of Alzheimer's and Parkinson's diseases by targeting specific protein aggregation pathways in the brain.

Key Programs:

  • • VT-201: Tau aggregation inhibitor (Phase I)
  • • VT-202: Alpha-synuclein modulator (Preclinical)
  • • VT-203: Neuroinflammation target (Discovery)
Pipeline Status: 1 candidate entering Phase I trials in Q3.

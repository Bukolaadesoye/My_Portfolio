Our Story

Pioneering Sustainable Agriculture Since 2010

Founded by agricultural engineers and environmental scientists, AgriNova Farms emerged from a shared vision: to transform modern agriculture into a force for environmental restoration and food security.

Our journey began with a single experimental farm in California's Central Valley. Today, we operate across 12 states and partner with over 200 farms nationwide, implementing our proven sustainable practices.

Our Mission

To revolutionize food production through technology-driven sustainable practices that benefit farmers, consumers, and the planet.

What We Offer

Innovative Farming for a Better Planet

Precision Ag Tech

Using IoT sensors and drone mapping to optimize irrigation, fertilization, and crop health, maximizing efficiency and minimizing waste.

  • Real-time soil moisture monitoring
  • Automated nutrient delivery systems
  • Drone-based crop health assessment

100% Organic Produce

Commitment to chemical-free farming. We offer certified organic fruits, vegetables, and grains, traceable from farm to table.

  • USDA Organic Certified
  • Non-GMO verified seeds
  • Full supply chain transparency

Advanced Water Management

Implementing advanced drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting systems to dramatically reduce water usage and environmental strain.

  • Smart irrigation scheduling
  • Rainwater collection & storage
  • Water recycling systems

Crop Consulting Services

Our team of agronomists provides personalized consulting to optimize your crop rotation, soil health, and pest management strategies.

Renewable Energy Integration

We help farms transition to solar and wind power, reducing operational costs and carbon footprint simultaneously.

Our Core Philosophy

Redefining the Relationship Between Humans and the Earth

At AgriNova, we believe that innovation and sustainability go hand-in-hand. We partner with local communities and researchers to constantly refine our approach.
